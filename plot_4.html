This is visualization #4. Here we can see a difference in Latitude vs Windspeed!

With this data we can make a few observations. We can see that there is not a very strong relationship between these 2 variables. However, we are able to see that, as latitude increases wind becomes a more regular occurance. Not only does wind become more regular at higher latitudes, but on average the wind becomes somewhat stronger.
